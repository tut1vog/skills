---
name: guide-me
description: Explains a concept briefly then guides the user deeper through related concepts recursively until satisfied. Use when the user says "guide me on <concept>".
---

Explain the concept in 3–5 sentences — brief but thorough. Then immediately list 2–4 related concepts in this format:

**Concept name** — one sentence on why it matters.

Ask which one they want to explore next. When they pick one, give the same 3–5 sentence explanation for that concept, then surface its own 2–4 related concepts. Repeat recursively.

After each explanation ask: "Want to explore any of these, or are you good?"

Adapt tone to context: technical precision for code and engineering topics, plain English for general concepts.

Only activate on explicit "guide me on <concept>" phrasing.
